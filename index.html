U Tube is a physical-digital hybrid tool that transforms how we brainstorm—no more talking over each other, awkward silences, or passive participants. Designed for teams who value creativity and clarity, U Tube uses a smart turntable to randomly assign turns, giving everyone equal voice. Once selected, a participant gets exclusive mic access and visible speaking time, shown via dynamic LED lighting. This ensures orderly discussion and encourages thoughtful, time-aware contributions. But it doesn’t stop there—U Tube adds a playful layer of interaction through custom “feedback tubes.” Teammates can swing their tube to extend a speaker’s time (+3s) or pull it to show doubt (–3s). These intuitive gestures allow for real-time reactions without verbal interruption, boosting engagement and idea refinement. Built with embedded systems like Raspberry Pi and Arduino, U Tube integrates audio control, gesture recognition, and visual cues into one seamless experience. The headset-microphone combo eliminates cross-talk, while tactile controls like squeeze-to-start make it feel natural and fun. Whether in classrooms, design studios, or boardrooms, U Tube helps teams stay focused, fair, and inspired. It’s not just a tool—it’s a new rhythm for collaborative thinking. U Tube: where structure meets spontaneity, and every idea gets its moment.
